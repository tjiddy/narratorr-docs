## What Narratorr Does

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Search Indexers" icon="magnifier">
    Search multiple indexers simultaneously — Torznab, Newznab, or import from Prowlarr. Torrents and Usenet, your choice.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Grab Releases" icon="rocket">
    Send downloads to qBittorrent, Transmission, SABnzbd, or NZBGet. Quality gates filter and rank results automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discover New Books" icon="star">
    Recommendation engine suggests new audiobooks based on your library — by author, series, genre, and narrator.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organize Your Library" icon="open-book">
    Configurable folder and file naming, ID3 tagging, cover art embedding. Your audiobooks, organized the way you want.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Import Lists" icon="list-format">
    Automatically add books from Audiobookshelf, NYT Bestsellers, or Hardcover. RSS feeds catch new releases in minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Processing" icon="setting">
    Merge multi-file downloads into a single M4B or MP3 using ffmpeg. Set bitrate, format, and merge behavior.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications" icon="email">
    Get notified via webhooks, Discord, or custom scripts when downloads grab, complete, import, or fail.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Self-Hosted" icon="laptop">
    Docker or bare metal. Automated backups, recycling bin, health checks. Your data stays on your hardware.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
