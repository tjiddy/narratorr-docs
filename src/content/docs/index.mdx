<div class="nr-section-label">Core Features</div>

<div class="nr-features">
  <div class="nr-feature-card">
    <div class="nr-feature-number">01</div>
    <h3>Search Indexers</h3>
    <p>Query Torznab, Newznab, and Prowlarr simultaneously. Torrents and Usenet — both protocols, one search.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nr-feature-card">
    <div class="nr-feature-number">02</div>
    <h3>Quality Gates</h3>
    <p>Set grab floors, reject words, seeder minimums. Every release is scored and ranked before it touches your library.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nr-feature-card">
    <div class="nr-feature-number">03</div>
    <h3>Discovery Engine</h3>
    <p>Recommendations powered by your library — matching authors, series gaps, narrators, and genres. With filter-bubble prevention built in.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nr-feature-card">
    <div class="nr-feature-number">04</div>
    <h3>Import Lists</h3>
    <p>Sync from Audiobookshelf, NYT Bestsellers, or Hardcover. RSS feeds catch new releases within minutes of upload.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nr-feature-card">
    <div class="nr-feature-number">05</div>
    <h3>Audio Processing</h3>
    <p>Merge multi-file downloads into a single M4B. Transcode, set bitrate, embed ID3 tags and cover art — all automatic.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nr-feature-card">
    <div class="nr-feature-number">06</div>
    <h3>Self-Hosted</h3>
    <p>Docker or bare metal. Automated backups, recycling bin, health checks, real-time updates. Your data, your hardware.</p>
  </div>
</div>
