## What Narratorr Does

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Search Indexers" icon="magnifier">
    Search multiple indexers simultaneously — Torznab, Newznab, AudioBookBay, or import from Prowlarr. Torrents and Usenet, your choice.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Grab Releases" icon="rocket">
    Send downloads to qBittorrent, Transmission, SABnzbd, or NZBGet. Narratorr monitors progress and imports when complete.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organize Your Library" icon="open-book">
    Configurable folder and file naming with token-based templates. Your audiobooks, organized the way you want them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Processing" icon="setting">
    Optionally merge multi-file downloads into a single M4B or MP3 using ffmpeg. Set bitrate, format, and merge behavior.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications" icon="email">
    Get notified via webhooks, Discord, or custom scripts when downloads grab, complete, import, or fail.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Self-Hosted" icon="laptop">
    Docker or bare metal. Your data stays on your hardware. No accounts, no cloud dependencies, no tracking.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
